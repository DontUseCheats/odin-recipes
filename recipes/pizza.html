Pizza Recipe

Homepage

Description

This homemade Deluctable easy to make pizza recipe is sure to warm the house and have it smelly pizzalicious!
We'll need nothing else other than an oven, oven mitts, pizza tray, ingredients and plates to serve the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pepperoni's
  • Low fat cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Spinach
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 385 degree's
  2. Pan out pizza dough to fit pizza tray mold
  3. Ladle pizza sauce starting from center and leave 1 inch of room on edges for the crust
  4. Sprinkle cheese to hearts content
  5. Lay out Pepperoni's evenly on both sides
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper moderately all over
  7. Place pizza in oven and wait 20 minutes
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
