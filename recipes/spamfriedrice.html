Spam Fried Rice Recipe

Homepage

Description

This recipe calls for a quick and easy 10 minute Spam Fried Rice!!

Ingredients

  • Old refrigerated white rice
  • Cooking oil
  • Spam
  • Eggs
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Preheat pan
  2. Dice up spam into small cubes
  3. Pour cooking oil
  4. Put rice in
  5. Wait till golden brown
  6. Pour raw egg in and mix
  7. Pour scallions on
  8. Serve for everyone!
